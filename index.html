About Us

COLLINS ARALU FOUNDATION Facilitate the development of our local communities in nigeria and in africa at laerge through an inclusive community-base food sustainability project. We are out to help our local communities irrespective of gender or religion. We make positive influence in the communities through empowering the society. We work for society where PEACE AND EQUITY will flourish. K & C Foundation fosters holistic mentorship and human development without any arrier or financial hindrance

With years of experience in the industry, our team is dedicated to helping you achieve your goals through innovative solutions tailored to your needs. Join us on this journey to excellence.

Our Mission

Our vision is to promote healthy society and a peaceful world at large by empowering children and youth, the less privileged and orphans and widows through community-based sustainability projects, skills, quality education, mentorship and scholarship, equity and diversity. We promote the restoration of our local communities, create job opportunities, provide healthy food system and with our collective community efforts we create sustainable environment around us through promotion of agriculture.
